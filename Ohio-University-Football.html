Ohio University Football

In February of 2023, I was hired to be a Graphic Designer for Ohio University's football program. This job has provided me with the opportunity to better understand how to design within a specific set of guidelines, all while giving me the creative freedom to experiment and make any composition I would like. For the 2023-2024 highschool football season, I created around 12 "Good Luck" graphics that were sent out weekly to recruits. 

Colt McManis
