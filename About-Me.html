About Me

I am Colt McManis and I am a Sports Graphic Designer. I was raised in Southern Ohio and I grew up obsessed with sports, not only the elements of the game, but what went into forming a team.

I am a senior at Ohio University majoring in Graphic Design with a minor in Marketing. Throughout undergrad, I have worked as a Graphic Designer for Ohio University Football while also working part-time as a Retail Sales Associate with Sherwin Williams. This past summer, I interned as a Graphic Designer for the St. Louis Surge, a women's professional basketball team. Combining my experience as a Graphic Designer, Marketing Minor, and Sales Associate, I have acquired strengths in adaptability, communication, creativity, and precision. 

Every day in my life provides me with the opportunity to learn. All of the moments in my jobs, classes, and conversations come together to strengthen not only my ability as a Graphic Designer, but also my collaboration skills and work ethic. These strengths I possess have assisted in leading me to where I am now, where I have the privilege of designing with my passion of sports. Those moments that transcend the game itself are what create lasting memories for the fans, and these follow us for the rest of out lives. They're truly what keep us all together. For me, it's not just about creating visually appealing designs; it's about capturing the essence of those unforgettable moments and translating them into a visual language.

My journey through the world of sports so far has allowed me to blend my passion with every project. As a designer I not only have the opportunity to represent a team with my creativity and precision, but also be an integral part of the moments fans will carry with them forever. The moments that–when I was growing up–lead me to where I am now.
